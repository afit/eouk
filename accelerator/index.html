What is the EO Accelerator Programme?

  • An Entrepreneurs' Organization program developed to help catapult entrepreneurs businesses from first to second-stage

    • Uncovering Strengths and Weaknesses
    • Sales and Marketing Day
    • Stages of closing a sale
    • Building lead generation and referrals
    • Core values and clients
